# Business Insights

## Insight 1 – Sales Trend

**Observation:** Monthly sales remain relatively stable throughout the year with slight peaks around February and October.

**Data Evidence:** The Sales Trend View line chart shows sales ranging approximately between ₹16M and ₹20M per month.

**Business Interpretation:** The business experiences consistent sales performance with minor seasonal fluctuations.

**Recommended Action:** Investigate the factors driving higher sales in peak months and replicate successful campaigns during lower-performing months.

---

## Insight 2 – Regional Performance

**Observation:** The South region generates the highest sales among all regions.

**Data Evidence:** The Regional Performance chart shows South with the tallest sales bar.

**Business Interpretation:** South is the strongest performing market and contributes significantly to overall revenue.

**Recommended Action:** Continue investing in South while identifying strategies to improve sales in East and West regions.

---

## Insight 3 – Category Profitability

**Observation:** Technology products generate substantially higher profit than Furniture and Office Supplies.

**Data Evidence:** The Category Profitability chart shows Technology sub-categories with the highest profit bars.

**Business Interpretation:** Technology products provide better profit margins and stronger business value.

**Recommended Action:** Increase inventory, promotions, and marketing efforts for high-performing technology products.

---

## Insight 4 – Customer Segment Behavior

**Observation:** Home Office customers contribute the highest sales, followed closely by Consumer and Corporate segments.

**Data Evidence:** The Customer Segment chart shows Home Office with the largest sales value.

**Business Interpretation:** The Home Office segment represents an important revenue source.

**Recommended Action:** Develop personalized marketing campaigns and loyalty programs targeting Home Office customers.

---

## Insight 5 – Discount Impact

**Observation:** Higher discount levels are associated with lower profitability.

**Data Evidence:** The Discount vs Profit scatter plot indicates profit decreases as discount percentage increases.

**Business Interpretation:** Excessive discounting negatively impacts business profitability.

**Recommended Action:** Review discount policies and focus on targeted promotional offers instead of blanket discounts.

---

## Insight 6 – Shipping Performance

**Observation:** Standard Class shipments have the highest average delivery time compared to premium shipping modes.

**Data Evidence:** The Shipping Performance chart shows Standard Class with the greatest average delivery days.

**Business Interpretation:** Longer delivery times may affect customer satisfaction and repeat purchases.

**Recommended Action:** Improve logistics efficiency for Standard Class deliveries or encourage customers to select faster shipping options.

---

## Insight 7 – Return Pattern

**Observation:** Furniture products experience the highest number of returns among all categories.

**Data Evidence:** The Return Analysis chart shows Furniture with the largest stacked return bar.

**Business Interpretation:** High return rates may indicate product quality issues or customer expectation gaps.

**Recommended Action:** Analyze return reasons, improve quality control, and enhance product descriptions.

---

## Insight 8 – Business Risk & Opportunity

**Observation:** The business demonstrates strong sales performance but profitability is sensitive to discounts and product returns.

**Data Evidence:** High sales are accompanied by lower profitability in heavily discounted products and higher returns in Furniture.

**Business Interpretation:** Revenue growth alone is insufficient without controlling operational costs and return rates.

**Recommended Action:** Focus on profitable product categories, optimize pricing strategies, reduce unnecessary discounts, and monitor return trends regularly.
